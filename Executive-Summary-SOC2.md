## Critical Issues Requiring Board Awareness

**1. Compromised Service Account (Resolved)**  
A service account credential leaked via a GitHub commit in December 2023 was exploited by a threat actor in July 2024. The attacker accessed internal log archives. No customer data was compromised. Immediate containment actions were taken and systemic fixes are in place (Secrets Manager, pre-commit hooks).

**2. Vendor Risk Assessment Program (In Progress)**  
NexaCloud does not have a formal third-party risk assessment process beyond collecting SOC 2 reports. This is a SOC 2 control gap. A vendor risk questionnaire program will be implemented by 31 October 2024.
