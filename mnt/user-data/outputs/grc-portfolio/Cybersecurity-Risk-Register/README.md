# Cybersecurity Risk Register — NexaCloud Solutions

**Framework:** NIST CSF 2.0, ISO 27001:2022  
**Risk Period:** FY2024  
**Document Owner:** GRC Manager  
**Reviewed By:** CISO  
**Last Updated:** September 2024

---

## Project Summary

This project contains a 15-risk enterprise cybersecurity risk register for NexaCloud Solutions, complete with heat map concept, executive dashboard, and KPI/KRI metrics. It uses a 5x5 likelihood-impact scoring methodology and tracks both inherent and residual risk.

---

## How This Demonstrates GRC Skills

- Constructing and maintaining an enterprise risk register with quantitative scoring
- Differentiating inherent vs residual risk and articulating treatment rationale
- Communicating risk posture to executive stakeholders through dashboards and KPI reporting
- Mapping risk to NIST CSF functions (Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover)

---

## Resume Bullet Points

- Built and maintained enterprise cybersecurity risk register with 15 identified risks; presented residual risk posture to CISO and Board quarterly
- Designed risk heat map and executive dashboard tracking inherent vs residual risk trends; enabled data-driven security investment decisions
- Defined and tracked 6 Key Risk Indicators (KRIs) aligned to NIST CSF functions
